Buon Cibo

Discover Italy's culinary soul at Buon Cibo, nestled in the heart of Asheville. Our passion for authentic flavors shines in every dish, from handcrafted pastas to wood-fired pizzas. With locally sourced ingredients and warm hospitality, we invite you to savor the essence of Italian cuisine at our table. Welcome to Buon Cibo.

Featured Dishes

carbonara

A classic Roman pasta dish made with spaghetti, eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It's creamy, savory, and comforting.

linguine alle vongole

Linguine pasta cooked with clams, garlic, white wine, parsley, and a touch of chili flakes. It's a flavorful seafood pasta that's light and aromatic.

caprese salad

A refreshing salad made with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, olive oil, balsamic glaze, salt, and pepper. It's a simple yet delightful starter.
