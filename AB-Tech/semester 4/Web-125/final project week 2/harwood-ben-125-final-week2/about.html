About us

Welcome to Buon Cibo, a cherished culinary gem born from the rich heritage of Italian immigrants who brought the soul of Italy to the heart of Asheville in the 1950s. Our story is woven with generations of tradition and a dedication to authenticity, echoing the flavors and warmth of Italian kitchens. With roots firmly planted in the old-world recipes passed down through our family, Buon Cibo is a testament to the enduring passion for Italian cuisine. From the bustling streets of Italy to the vibrant tables of Asheville, our restaurant stands as a testament to the timeless traditions and the enduring love for sharing exquisite flavors and heartfelt hospitality with our community.
